# Supabase Setup - Follow These Steps

STEP 1: Go to https://supabase.com
        - Sign in with Google or GitHub
        - Click "Start your project" if new
        - Click "New project" button

STEP 2: Fill in project details:
        Name: fifi-hair-salon
        Database Password: [CREATE STRONG PASSWORD - SAVE IT!]
        Region: East US (N. Virginia) or closest to you
        Plan: Free

STEP 3: Click "Create new project"
        Wait 2-3 minutes for setup to complete

STEP 4: Get your credentials:
        - Click Settings icon (left sidebar)
        - Click "API" section
        - Copy these values:
          * Project URL: https://xxxxx.supabase.co
          * anon public key: eyJhbGc...
          * service_role key: eyJhbGc...

STEP 5: Create database table:
        - Click "SQL Editor" in left sidebar
        - Click "New query"
        - Copy the SQL from DATABASE_READY.md
        - Paste and click "Run"

STEP 6: Update .env.local with your credentials
